Introduction

The utility I will be reviewing is CCleaner. This utility was made on September 23, 2003. It was created by the developer Priform. CCleaner was initially released on the Windows operating system and was later brought to IOS. This utility was created to clean unwanted files and invalid windows registry to clear out storage space for computers.

Download Instructions

  1. Go to the CCleaner website: https://www.ccleaner.com/
  2. Find the free version of CCleaner and click the green download button.
  3. There will be 3 different versions of CCleaner on the page, go look under the free section and press the download button again.
  4. After the download finishes, follow these steps to install it.

General Usage

The general usage of CCleaner as I explained in the intro is mainly used for deleting files that are not needed on your computer. There are different levels of the utility you are able to get. If you are willing to pay for it then you will gain access to other features like cleaning everything on your computer or file recovery that the free version does not have.

Pros and Cons

Pros
  • Takes up small amount of space
  • Fast and easy install
  • Clears more space for your computer
Cons
  • Free version is very limited with the action you are able to do

Alternative utilities like CCleaner

There are other utilities like CCleaner such as: Wise Disk Cleaner, Clean Master, and System Ninja.
